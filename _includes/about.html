About

A brief history of Third Monday, LLC.

  • 2003-2005

    Humble Beginnings

    Third Monday, LLC was established by our founders to do independent consulting work after the demise of the company that employed them. The company name is a reference to the monthly dividend delivery date for investors of a local brewery.

  • March 2005

    A Significant Project

    Third Monday, LLC signed a significant contract to build an advanced neuro-technology software solution that would eventually become the core product for our client.

  • August 2010

    Into Hibernation

    Our neuro-technology client hired us full-time, effectively placing Third Monday, LLC into a hibernation state. Although no longer pursuing new business, Third Monday, LLC has continued to support a few small customers.

  • August 2020

    An Unexpected Twist

    The pandemic brought about a rather sudden change for Third Monday, LLC, and our founders have been separately engaged for several years.

  • November 2024

    The Next Chapter

    We're excited to breathe new life into Third Monday, LLC by returning to our independent work with small and mid-size businesses to help them craft some really cool software solutions.
